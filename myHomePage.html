My story

From nursing to
cybersecurity — the long way.

Hi, I'm Niya! My path into IT wasn't a straight line. I started as a nursing major, pivoted to cybersecurity at St. John's University, and now I'm studying IT with a network security specialization at NJIT. I built this site because I wished something like it existed when I started.

Every detour taught me something. And now I want to share every resource, shortcut, and lesson with the next generation of women in tech.
